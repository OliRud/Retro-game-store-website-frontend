Who We Are

We are an independent retro game store based in the UK, dedicated to keeping the golden age of gaming alive! Our shelves are stocked with a treasure trove of classic titles and consoles, from the iconic Atari and NES to the beloved Sega Genesis and beyond. Whether you're a seasoned gamer looking for a nostalgic trip down memory lane, or a curious newcomer eager to experience the classics for the first time, we offer something for everyone. We're passionate about all things retro gaming, and our friendly staff is always happy to chat about your favourite games, offer recommendations, or help you find that hidden gem you've been searching for.

Our Goal

Our goal is to be your one-stop shop for all things retro gaming!

We strive to keep the golden age of gaming alive by:


  • Preserving gaming history: We believe retro games are a valuable part of gaming history. By offering them a platform, we hope to introduce them to new generations and ensure they're not forgotten.
  • Curating a diverse selection of classic titles and consoles:We source a wide range of retro games, from the most popular franchises to those hidden gems you might have missed. We stock consoles across generations, ensuring there's something for every taste and budget.
  • Providing a seamless experience: We want your retro gaming experience to be smooth sailing. We offer cleaning and repair services for consoles, and can help you track down missing manuals or controllers.
  • Fostering a welcoming community:Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just starting out, we want our store to be a place where you feel comfortable. Our staff is passionate about retro gaming and loves discussing all things old-school.
